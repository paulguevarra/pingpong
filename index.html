How It Works

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 to your chosen number

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with Ping
    • example: "1, 2, 3, 4"
    • becomes: "1, 2, PING, 4"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with PONG
    • example: "49, 50, 51"
    • becomes: "49,PONG, 51"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with PING-PONG
    • example: "13, 14, 15, 16"
    • becomes: "13, 14, PING-PONG, 16"
  4. All other numbers remain normal
